Bonus_Shapes	Bonus Shapes
Wall	Gets in the way; can only be destroyed with a Bomb.
GhostStar	Merges with another Star, but disappears after you drop 5 shapes.
Multiply	Merges with only itself to create a Star.
Inverter	Changes the gravity of all shapes.
Portal	Moves a shape to the opposite side of the level.
Bag	Creates multiple cursed Circles. (Cursed shapes can't merge with other cursed shapes.)
Egg	Transforms into a Star after dropping 6 shapes, but disappears if you score any points.
Snowflake	Any shape that is surrounding this cannot be merged or destroyed. Merge 5 shapes to destroy this.
Duplicate	Curses the first shape it touches and then transforms into a cursed copy of that shape. (Cursed shapes can't merge with other cursed shapes.)
Chest	Transforms the first 4 Squares it touches into Arrows.
